Gather Documents

Step 3: Collect All Necessary Documents

Universities typically require you to upload or mail several supporting documents. These often include:

  • Official transcripts from your high school or university
  • Letters of recommendation from teachers, professors, or employers
  • A professional CV/Resume highlighting achievements
  • Proof of language proficiency test results (TOEFL/IELTS)

Make sure to scan and save everything in both PDF and physical copies, as some institutions ask for hard copies later.
