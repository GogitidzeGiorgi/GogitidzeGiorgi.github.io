Step 6: Visa Application Process

Once you have your admission letter, you’ll need to apply for a student visa. The process usually involves:

  • Filling out the official visa application form
  • Paying visa fees
  • Submitting passport, photos, admission letters, and financial proof
  • Scheduling and attending a visa interview at the embassy or consulate

Start this process early, as visa approvals can take several weeks.
