Step 3: Deadlines and Tracking

Maintain a consolidated deadline calendar to ensure you never miss an application cutoff.


  • USA: Early Action/Decision (Oct-Nov), Regular Decision (Dec-Jan), rolling admissions
  • EU: UCAS - Oxbridge/Medicine (Oct 15), other courses (Jan 31), clearing (Jun-Jul); national portals (Feb-Apr)
  • Use tools like Google Sheets, Trello, or BridgeU to set reminders 4-6 weeks in advance
  • Update your calendar as you receive conditional offer deadlines
