Step 8: Receive and Evaluate Offers

When admission decisions arrive, review each offer’s terms carefully before confirming your place.


  • Distinguish between unconditional offers and conditional offers (e.g., pending final transcripts or language scores)
  • Examine program details, tuition fees, scholarship awards, and any stipulated conditions
  • Note acceptance deadlines and deposit requirements in your calendar
  • Compare offers holistically—rankings, funding, location, career services—and accept or decline via portal
