Once you have your admission letter, you'll need to apply for a student visa.

Step 9: Apply for Student Visa & Consular Process

Begin your visa application early to allow sufficient processing time.


  • USA (F-1/J-1): obtain I-20/DS-2019 → complete DS-160 → pay SEVIS fee → schedule and attend embassy interview
  • EU (D-Type visa): submit national visa form → proof of funds (~€10 000/year or blocked account) → health insurance → embassy appointment
  • Compile all required documents: acceptance letter, bank statements, proof of ties to home country
  • Apply 2-3 months before your program start date to avoid delays
