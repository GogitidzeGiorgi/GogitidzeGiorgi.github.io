Step 6: Finance Studies & Scholarships

Plan your funding strategy in parallel with your application process to meet both admission and scholarship deadlines.


  • University aid: merit- or need-based scholarships, teaching/research assistantships (USA grad)
  • External funding: Fulbright/Muskie (USA); Erasmus + grants, DAAD, Eiffel (FR), Chevening (UK)
  • Prepare financial statements, tax forms, and scholarship essays as required
  • Track separate scholarship deadlines (often precede admission dates)
