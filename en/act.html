ACT® Test Content

An overview of the test sections, timing, and tasks.

Test overview

The ACT has four required multiple-choice sections: English, Math, Reading, and Science. The total test takes 2 hours and 55 minutes to complete (without the optional Writing section), but you should plan for about 3.5 hours to allow for check-in and breaks. There is also an optional Writing section which takes an additional 40 minutes.

Test sections overview

Use the table below to view the breakdown of each ACT test section.

Section Estimated Timing Questions/Tasks Description
English 45 minutes 75 questions Measures understanding of English conventions like grammar and punctuation, as well as rhetorical skills.
Math 60 minutes 60 questions Covers pre-algebra, algebra, geometry, and trigonometry. A calculator is permitted for the entire section.
Reading 35 minutes 40 questions Assesses your ability to read closely, determine central ideas, and understand meaning in several prose passages.
Science 35 minutes 40 questions Measures interpretation, analysis, and problem-solving skills using passages, graphs, and tables from the natural sciences.
Writing (Optional) 40 minutes 1 essay Measures your writing skills by asking you to respond to a prompt that presents an issue and three different perspectives.
