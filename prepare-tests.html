Step 2: Test Preparation

Register and prepare for all required language and academic exams. Start at least three months before your earliest application deadline to achieve your best results.


  • Check which exams (TOEFL, IELTS, GRE, GMAT) are required by your target universities
  • Schedule and take exams, that are required by your target universities (Check requirements)
  • Register for SAT/ACT (USA undergrad) or GRE/GMAT (USA graduate) as needed
  • Build a study plan with practice resources and set aside time for potential retakes
  • Confirm score-reporting procedures and institution codes
