Write Essays

Step 4: Write Strong Essays & Personal Statements

Your essays are one of the most important parts of your application. They show admissions officers who you are beyond grades and test scores.

  • Personal Statement: Highlight your journey, values, and goals.
  • Motivation Letter: Explain why you chose a particular program or country.
  • Supplemental Essays: Answer specific prompts given by universities.

Draft early, revise multiple times, and get feedback from mentors or peers before submission.
