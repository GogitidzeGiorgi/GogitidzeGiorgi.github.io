Step 5: Submit Your University Applications

After preparing your documents and essays, it’s time to fill in the application forms.

  • Double-check all entered information before submitting.
  • Upload required documents in the correct format.
  • Pay attention to application deadlines—late submissions are often not accepted.
  • Keep track of all application portals and confirmation receipts.

It’s a good idea to apply to a mix of “reach,” “match,” and “safety” schools.
