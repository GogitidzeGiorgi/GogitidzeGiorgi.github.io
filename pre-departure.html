Step 7: Pre-departure Preparations

Congratulations! You’ve made it through the application and visa process. Now prepare for your journey:

  • Book your flight tickets early to get the best prices.
  • Arrange accommodation—on-campus housing or off-campus rentals.
  • Attend university orientation programs, if available.
  • Check health insurance requirements and vaccinations.
  • Pack wisely—don’t forget essential documents and medications.

Stay connected with international student groups for support before and after arrival.
